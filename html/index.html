About Our Ministry

Spreading God's word through the airwaves

Grace Waves Christian Radio was founded with a vision to spread God's word and love through the airwaves, reaching hearts and homes with uplifting content that encourages, inspires, and strengthens faith.

"How beautiful are the feet of those who bring good news!" Romans 10:15

Our mission is to provide quality Christian programming that ministers to the spiritual needs of our listeners. Through music, teaching, and inspirational messages, we aim to be a source of encouragement and spiritual growth for believers and a welcoming introduction to the Christian faith for seekers.

With a dedicated team of hosts, producers, and volunteers, we work diligently to create content that glorifies God and serves our community.
